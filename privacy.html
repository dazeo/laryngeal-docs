Privacy Policy — Laryngeal

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Laryngeal does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies of any kind, and it does not require an account, sign-in, or network connection to function.

Audio

Laryngeal generates all sound on-device through audio synthesis. It does not record, access, or transmit audio from your microphone.

Third parties

Laryngeal does not integrate any third-party SDK that collects data.

Children

Laryngeal does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this same URL with a revised date.

Contact

Questions about this policy: xinqichaw@gmail.com

プライバシーポリシー — Laryngeal

最終更新日:2026年7月14日

Laryngealは、個人情報を収集・保存・送信しません。アナリティクス、広告識別子、トラッキングの類は一切使用しておらず、アプリの利用にアカウント登録・ログイン・通信環境は必要ありません。
